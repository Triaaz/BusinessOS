# BusinessOS

BusinessOS is a full-stack business management platform built to help small and medium-sized businesses manage their operations from a single, centralized dashboard.

Instead of juggling multiple tools for inventory, sales, customers, suppliers, and business records, BusinessOS brings everything together into one scalable platform.

This project also serves as my flagship engineering project, where I'm applying software engineering principles, system design, backend development, frontend development, and eventually DevOps practices to build production-ready software.

---

## Features

### Authentication & Access

* Secure User Authentication
* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
* Business Owner & Staff Accounts

### Business Management

* Inventory Management
* Product & Category Management
* Customer Management
* Supplier Management

### Sales

* Sales & Order Management
* Invoice Generation
* Stock Movement Tracking
* Inventory History

### Business Insights

* Expense Tracking
* Dashboard Analytics
* Reports *(Planned)*
